Temporal Coherence Gravity

Version 0.1 | Beverly Scientific | June 2026

This website presents an exploratory framework investigating whether causality, time, spacetime, and gravitational phenomena may emerge from underlying temporal coherence dynamics.

Abstract

Modern physics successfully describes gravitational phenomena through General Relativity and quantum phenomena through Quantum Mechanics. However, significant conceptual gaps remain between these frameworks.

Temporal Coherence Gravity explores the possibility that causality is more fundamental than spacetime geometry and that coherent temporal organization may provide a common foundation for the emergence of time, spacetime structure, and gravitational behavior.

The framework is exploratory and intended to generate testable predictions rather than replace existing theories.

Origin of the Project

This project did not begin as an attempt to develop a theory of gravity. It originated from questions concerning information preservation, black holes, statistical emergence, causality, and the nature of time. Over time these ideas converged into a single question:

Could causality, time, and spacetime emerge from the organization of underlying informational relationships rather than existing as fundamental structures?

Disclaimer

Temporal Coherence Gravity is an exploratory research framework. The ideas presented here are speculative and should not be interpreted as established scientific conclusions. The purpose of the project is to provide a mathematically motivated framework that generates testable predictions and encourages further investigation.
